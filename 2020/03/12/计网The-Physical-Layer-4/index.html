Digital Modulation and Multiplexing

Signal Encoding Techniques

  • Digital data, digital signal
  • Digital data, analog signal
  • Analog data, digital signal
  • Analog data, analog signal

Baseband transmission vs Passband transmission

  • Baseband signal:Signals that run from 0 up to a maximum frequency.

  • Passband signal:Signals that are shifted to occupy a higher range of frequencies.

    eg.wireless transmissions

  • Baseband transmission: In which the signals occupies frequencies from 0 to a maximum frequency.

  • Passband transmission: In which the signals occupies a band of frequencies around the frequency of the carrier signal.

在一条传输介质上不能传两路基带信号，原因是两路基带信号的频率重叠，会互相干扰

在一条传输介质上可以可能同时传两路通带信号，只要两路通带信号频率不相互重叠

基带信号一般用于短距离传输，不是很常用.

通带信号一般用于长距离传输，很常用和常见.

Baseband transmission

  • NRZ不归零码.
  • 曼彻斯特码.
  • AMI码.

以上三种码是重点研究对象的码.

光纤不能同时传正负信号.即这种AMI码.

Manchester

  • Transition in middle of each bit period
  • Transition serves as clock and data
  • Low to high represents 1,High to low represents 0
  • Used by IEEE 802.3 LAN

Modulation

  • Placing a signal in a given frequency band is useful.

    • the frequency of transmission media does not start at zero.
    • for wireless channel: sending very low frequency signals is not practical.
    • for wires: different kinds of signals can coexist on one channel.

  • Digital modulation is accomplished by modulating a carries signal sits in the passband.

  • modulate the amplitude(振幅)，frequency(频率)，or phase(相值) of the carrier signal.
  • device: Modem.

以载波信号为基础.

(a) 原始二进制信号

(b)数字信号：幅移键控（ASK）. 模拟信号：调幅（AM）.

(c)调频（FSK）. 频移键控..疎密波

(d)相移键控（PSK）.

Combination of modulation techniques.

  • modems use a combination of modulation techniques to transmit multiple bits per baud.

    QPSK: quadrature phase shift keying-正交相移键控.

    QAM: quadrature amplitude modulation-正交幅度调制.

  • constellation diagram

    • the phase of dot is indicated by the angle of a line from it to the origin makes with the positive X-axis.
    • the amplitude of dot is the distance from the origin.

Multiplexing(复用)

  • refer to a process where multiple analog message signals or digital data streams are combined into one signal.
  • TDM(时分复用)
  • FDM(频分复用)
  • WDM(波分复用)
  • CDM(码分复用)
Frequency Division Multiplexing

  • (a) The original bandwidths.
  • (b) The bandwidths raised in frequency.
  • (c) The multiplexed channel.
Time Division Multiplexing
  • Signals are transferred apparently simultaneously as sub-channels in one communication channel，but are physically taking turns on the channel.
  • The time domain is divided into several recurrent timeslots of fixed length，one for each sub-channel.

TDM Frames(复用帧)
  • 同步时分复用

优点：接收方根据时隙就知道是哪个用户的数据，几个用户固定在不同的时隙.

缺点：容易造成资源浪费

同步时分复用的使用场合：每一路用户所有时间段都有数据. eg：电话网

Synchronous TDM vs. Statistical TDM
  • 统计时分复用或者异步时分复用

时隙和用户之间不做绑定关系，不同步. 为了让接收方知道哪个时隙是哪个用户的数据，通常要在传输的数据上绑定地址位.

一般来说，计算机就是用统计时分复用的方式进行传输信息.只要信道空闲，来的数据都能进行传输.

CDM(码分复用)
  • A form of spread(传播；扩展；扩散) spectrum (范围；光谱；波普；系列) communication
  • Code Division Multiple Access: Each station can transmit over the entire frequency spectrum all time
  • Multiple simultaneous transmissions are separated using coding theory.

所有的用户同时间同频率进行传输信息
